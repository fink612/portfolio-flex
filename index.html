Portfolio

Temp-O-Matic

The Temp-O-Matic is a programmable thermometer that allows the user to record the temperature of food cooling at customizable increments. Many restaurants and food businesses rely on certain proceedures to cool food quickly to avoid bacterial growth. This device takes a temperature reading every interval and records it to internal memory. It beeps and sends a notification to a computer via email or text message if the food is not on track to cool safely so other methods can be implemented.

Food Finder

Food Finder is a freely downloadable app that helps people with extra food find people that are hungry. In recent months, the COVID-19 pandemic has brought to light the issue of food insecurity in every neighborhood. Also, people that are hungry can't always afford technology or have the resources to find food giveaway events. Food Finder addresses these issues by connecting people that have just a small amount of food to share at the time of cooking.

EZ Inventory

Inventory management at a restaurant or deli can be a pain! That's why I invented EZ Inventory, an app that integrates a barcode database with your business' inventory spreadsheet. No more pen and paper tours followed by hours of computer work. How many times have you asked in frustration WHY ISN'T THERE AN APP FOR THIS??!? Well, now there is!
